## Indicators
- **(a)** Litmus Paper
	- Blue Litmus is used to test
		-  Acids(It turns Red)
	- Red Litmus is used to test
		- Bases (It turns Blue)
- **(b)** Thymolphthalein
	- Acid - Colorless
	- Strong Base Blue/purpulish
- **(c)** Methyl Orange
	- Acidic Medium - Red 
	- Basic medium - Yellow

**Examples**

- Weak Acids
	- Carboxylic Acids

## Oxides
- Metal Oxides
	- $\ce{Na2O}$, $\ce{MgO, CuO, CaO}$ 
	- **Base**
- Non Metal Oxides
	- $\ce{SO2}$ , $\ce{CO2}$ 
	- **Acids**

- **Amphoteric Oxides**
- They react. With both Acids and Bases
	- They Behave like Acids/Bases both
	- $\ce{Al2O3, ZnO}$
